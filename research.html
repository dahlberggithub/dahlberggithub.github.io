Research Interests

Welcome to my mathematical research page website. Showcased here are my collaborative projects with my outstanding coauthors and mentors. My work spans a variety of intriguing topics, including chromatic symmetric functions with a focus on e-positivity, graphs of reduced words of permutations, pattern avoidance in permutations and restricted growth functions, DP colorings, and the combinatorial Nullstellensatz.

Although not within the papers below, my interests also lie in exploring and using reinforcement learning, neural networks and general data analytics. On my GitHub page I have placed some code I have done in exploring complex combinatorial objects as well as an interesting project with a coding partner on using reinforcement learning to find extremal examples of non-e-positive graphs (coming soon).

Collaboration enriches research by bringing many amazing minds together and outputting possibilities beyond the individual. I am grateful for the many thrilling hours working on projects with these amazing partners.
