Mystery Mosaic

How it Works

You can make your own mystery mosaic here. All you will need to do is choose a picture and choose a difficulty. Keep in mind that this process is a little rudimentary, so images with clear colors will give better outputs, like cartoons.

All images used below are from Google Images with Creative Commons licenses.

Make Your Own

Upload your own picture here and choose your difficulty. You can see some samples below.

  • Easy mosaics are roughy 15 squares accross. It is best to use images with simple shapes with little to no outlines.
  • Medium mosaics are roughy 25 squares accross.
  • Hard mosaics are roughy 35 squares accross.
